Project Overview

The Caregivers Connection Platform is a web-based application designed to simplify the process of finding and hiring caregiving services. It provides an intuitive and efficient platform where individuals, families, and organizations can connect with qualified caregivers. The platform allows users to create detailed job postings based on specific caregiving needs, ensuring that service seekers can find the right match. Caregivers can register, build comprehensive profiles, and apply for available jobs directly through the system. To streamline the hiring process, an integrated appointment scheduling system enables users to book and manage caregiving sessions with ease.
